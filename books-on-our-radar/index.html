How books end up on this page

Books on Our Radar is a living collection of titles that genuinely caught our attention while exploring bookstores, talking with booksellers, and discovering new releases.

These are not reviews or rankings. Some books here are ones we've already read, while others are simply books we're excited to discover. The goal is to celebrate curiosity and the joy of finding your next great read.
