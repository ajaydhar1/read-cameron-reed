Overview

About this guide

This reader's guide is designed to be read alongside the novel. Each chapter guide discusses only the chapter in its title, so readers can explore summaries, themes, questions, and world-building notes without running into spoilers from later chapters.

The guide is being built gradually, one chapter at a time, with room for future pages on characters, technology, themes, and the novel's larger questions.
